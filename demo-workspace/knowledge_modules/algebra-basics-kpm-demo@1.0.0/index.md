# Algebra Basics

Welcome to **Algebra Basics** — a structured introduction to the core ideas of algebra that underpin all of mathematics and computer science.

Algebra is the study of symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. It generalises arithmetic by replacing specific numbers with variables, allowing statements that are true for entire classes of values.
